Kristina Ralston



Kristina Ralston is a personal development coach with over sixteen years of combined mindfulness practice, training, and coaching. She is passionate about how a Mostly Mindful Life is a path to greater self-awareness and authenticity, and seeks to empower all of her clients in discovering a truer sense of self and purpose. Kristina is an active social media contributor and thought leader, devourer of books, and loves cooking tasty food for people.
